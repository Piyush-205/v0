From the Heart

A letter written
just for you

My dearest,

There are a thousand things I want to say to you today, and somehow "Happy Anniversary" doesn't feel like nearly enough. But here I am, trying anyway — because you deserve every word.

I think about the person you are — how you make ordinary moments feel extraordinary just by being in them. The way you laugh, the way you care, the way you somehow always know exactly what I need. You are, without a doubt, one of the most beautiful things that has ever happened to me.

Another year has gone by, and I am more certain than ever that I want to spend every single one of them with you. Not just the easy days, but all of them — the tired evenings, the silly arguments, the quiet mornings. I want it all, as long as it's with you.

Thank you for choosing me, again and again. Thank you for being my home.

Forever yours, with all my love 💕

Here's to forever ❤️

Happy Anniversary · With all the love in the world
